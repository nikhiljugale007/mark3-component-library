What is neoG.camp?

"A bootcamp that revives the learning for new(neo) generation(+G) programmers." As said neoG.camp is a bootcamp that aims to make all students industry ready in web development by following unique approach of learn by dooing.

Who will be teaching us?

Tanay Pratap will be our teacher, mentor and guide in the whole bootcamp. He is a software engineer at Microsoft and a passionate teacher. He is teaching from 2014 on his youtube channel and given talks in many colleges, community talks and fests. He already helped many students in learning and getting jobs.
